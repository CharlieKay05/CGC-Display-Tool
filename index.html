Privacy & Data Notice

Before you
begin

APEX collects session data, scores, and photos to help teams evaluate in-store displays. Please read the key points on the right before continuing.

This tool is operated by Mars CGC, London Paddington.
Data is stored locally on this device and not transmitted to external servers.
Sessions can be exported and deleted at any time.
01 — Data & Privacy
🔒 What we collect
Session scores, display ratings, assessor notes, and timestamps are recorded during each assessment. No personal data about shoppers is collected. All data remains on this device unless you choose to export it.
02 — Photo Usage
📷 Photos taken in this tool
Photos captured are used solely to document display conditions for the session record. They are stored locally and included only in exports you initiate. Do not photograph people without consent.
03 — Access
👥 Who can see this data?
Only users with access to this device and any session exports you share. Exports should be handled in line with your organisation's data sharing guidelines.
By continuing, you confirm you have read this notice and agree to use APEX in accordance with your organisation's policies.
